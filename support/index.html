Quick answers

My birth time is unknown. Check "time unknown" during onboarding — we cast for noon. Planets stay accurate; houses and your rising sign become approximate.

How do I move to a new phone? More → Backup & restore. Push a backup, note your code, type it on the new device. Everything comes back.

How do I get my data out? More → Export. Full chart as text, free, forever. No account needed, nothing held hostage.

Cancel Premium? Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Cancelling keeps your charts and the free tier.
