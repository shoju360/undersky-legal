Privacy Policy

Last updated July 18, 2026

Undersky is built on a simple rule: your data is always yours.

What we collect

  • Birth information you enter — date, time, and city for you and the people you save. It is stored on your device and sent to our chart engine solely to compute charts and write your readings.
  • Nothing else. No contacts, no location tracking, no advertising identifiers, no analytics profiles tied to you.

What we do with it

  • Compute astrological charts (Swiss Ephemeris) and generate written readings.
  • If you turn on backup, your charts are stored in our vault under a random code that only you hold. Without the code, your data can't be found.
  • Your recent readings are retained by the engine so tomorrow's reading can remember yesterday's. They are keyed to your chart, not to any account or identity.

What we never do

  • We never sell or share your data with anyone.
  • We never lock your saved charts behind a paywall.
  • We never require an account.

Your controls

  • Export your full chart data as text anytime, free (More → Export).
  • Backup & restore with a code you control (More → Backup & restore).
  • Delete — removing the app removes all on-device data. To clear vault backups or reading history, email us your backup code and we will delete them.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Entertainment disclaimer

Undersky is for reflection and entertainment. Readings are not medical, legal, or financial advice.
